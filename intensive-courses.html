Intensive Courses

Pass Your Test, Fast

Our intensive driving courses, also known as crash courses, are designed for learners who need to get their licence quickly. Whether you have a job that requires driving, a holiday booked, or simply want to pass as soon as possible, we can tailor a course to your needs.

Unlike a 1-hour-per-week approach, intensive courses stack multiple hours of driving per day over a compressed period. This immersive method can dramatically accelerate your progress – many of our pupils pass within 1–3 weeks.

⚠️
Important

Intensive courses work best for confident learners with some prior experience. If you're a complete beginner, we recommend starting with a few regular lessons first to build basic skills.
